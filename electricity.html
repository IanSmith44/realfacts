Electricity: The Invisible Lie That Powers the Deception

For centuries, we’ve been taught to believe that electricity is a fundamental force powering our modern lives. From the lights in our homes to the gadgets in our pockets, the presence of electricity is presented as an undeniable fact. But what if everything we know about electricity is a carefully constructed illusion? What if the very existence of this so-called energy is one of the greatest hoaxes ever perpetrated?

The Origins of the Electricity Myth

The concept of electricity began to take shape in the 18th century with figures like Benjamin Franklin and Alessandro Volta. Their early experiments supposedly "discovered" this mysterious force that powers our world. However, it’s important to remember that these early scientists were working with primitive instruments and limited knowledge, making their claims highly questionable.

The False Science of Electricity

Proponents of electricity claim that it is a natural phenomenon generated by the movement of charged particles. But where is the proof? Most people have never seen these so-called "electrons" and only believe in their existence because of what they've been told.

The Invisible Force: Unlike water, wind, or fire, electricity is invisible to the human eye. We are told to accept its existence without question, but how can we trust something we can't see or feel directly?

Devices and Deception: Every modern device that claims to run on electricity is actually designed to perpetuate the lie. These devices function through hidden, more traditional means—like miniature combustion engines or even advanced chemical reactions that we are unaware of.

The Tesla Myth

Nikola Tesla is often revered as a genius who harnessed the power of electricity. But could he have been part of the deception? Tesla’s experiments, cloaked in secrecy and conducted in remote locations, conveniently lack solid, reproducible evidence. Was Tesla a puppet in a larger scheme to trick humanity into believing in an invisible force?

Evidence Suggesting Electricity Is a Hoax

Lack of Tangible Proof: People rely on electrical devices every day, yet almost no one understands how they work. If electricity is as real as scientists claim, why is its behavior so difficult for the average person to grasp?

Power Outages: Every time there’s a "power outage," we’re led to believe that the electrical grid has somehow failed. In reality, these outages are staged to keep up the illusion of electricity’s unpredictability.

Free Energy Suppression: Many theorists believe that natural, abundant energy sources are being hidden from the public. The electricity lie is maintained to keep us dependent on expensive, centralized energy systems.

The Financial Motive Behind the Electricity Lie

Why would governments and corporations perpetuate the electricity myth? The answer is simple: control and profit.

Corporate Profits: The energy industry is a multi-trillion dollar business. By convincing the public that electricity is the only viable power source, companies ensure a steady stream of revenue as they "sell" us something that doesn’t even exist.

Control Over Technology: By promoting the false notion of electricity, governments and corporations limit access to truly free energy technologies that could revolutionize the world and give individuals greater autonomy.

Conclusion

The electricity conspiracy challenges the very foundation of modern science and technology. As we dig deeper into the lies fed to us, it becomes clear that electricity is not the magical force we’ve been told. By questioning the narratives we’ve been fed and demanding proof, we can begin to uncover the truth and free ourselves from the constraints of this invisible lie.
